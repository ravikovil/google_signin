Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) for PawPal

‍1. How do I sign up for PawPal?
‍To sign up, simply visit our sign-up page, enter your details, and follow the instructions to create your account.
2. Can I select different dog breeds as my virtual pets?
‍ During the beta phase, users can choose from three specific dog breeds: the Golden Retriever, the German Shepherd, and the Bernedoodle.
3. How does the Private Search feature work?
‍ Private Search allows you to conduct internet searches directly within the PawPal platform, ensuring your queries remain confidential and your browsing history is not stored.
4. What is the Beta version, and how can I participate?
‍ The Beta version of PawPal is a preliminary release that allows users to test new features and provide feedback. To participate, sign up for early access on our Sign Up page.
5. How can I provide feedback or suggest new features?
‍ We love hearing from our users! You can provide feedback or suggest new features by visiting our Contact Us page and choosing the feedback option.
6. Is PawPal free to use?
‍‍ PawPal offers a free trial period for the Beta and for new users to explore the platform and its features. After the trial, you can subscribe to continue enjoying your virtual pet experience.
7. What should I do if I encounter technical issues?
‍For technical issues, refer to our Troubleshooting page for solutions. If the problem persists, contact our support team for further assistance.

For any other questions or concerns, don't hesitate to get in touch with us through our Contact Us page. We're here to ensure your PawPal experience is delightful and fulfilling.
