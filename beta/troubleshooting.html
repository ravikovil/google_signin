Troubleshooting

Encountering a hiccup with your PawPal? No worries! Our comprehensive troubleshooting guide is here to help you resolve technical issues and app glitches swiftly, ensuring your journey with your virtual pet remains smooth and enjoyable. Follow our step-by-step instructions to quickly find solutions and get back to your PawPal adventures.
Common Issues and Solutions:

App Not Loading or Crashing:

● Check Your Internet Connection: Ensure you have a stable internet connection. Sometimes, a weak signal can interrupt your experience.
● Restart Your Device: Sometimes, a simple restart can clear up any minor glitches causing the app to crash.

App Not Loading or Crashing:

● Contact Support:
‍If issues persist, detailed descriptions of your problem can help our support team provide targeted solutions.

App Not Loading or Crashing:

● Reset Your Password:
If you’ve forgotten your password, use the 'Forgot Password' feature to reset it.
● Clear Cache: Clearing your app cache can solve login problems, especially if you're stuck on the login screen.

Still Need Help?

If you’ve followed these steps and still face issues, our dedicated support team is ready to assist you further. Reach out to us through the Contact Support section on our app or website, and we'll work diligently to resolve your issue.
